JVC - Powerful Sound On Ear Headphones - Black

JVC Powerful Sound lightweight wired on ear headphones offer a comfortable solution for everyday use. The HAS31MB offers an integrated microphone and one button remote on the headset. One button remote allows users to control phone calls (answer/end) and stop/start music in additon to changing tracks forward/back. Soft ear pads provide premium noise isolation. The single sided cord offers a 90 degree 3.5mm analog connector. Voice assistant compatible.
