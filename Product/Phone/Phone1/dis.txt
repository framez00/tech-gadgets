NanoPhone

Key Features:

Compact Size: Smaller than a credit card, measuring just 0.4" x 3.5" x 1.8" and weighing 2.3 ounces, making it highly portable and convenient.

Connectivity: Supports 4G and Wi-Fi, allowing for clear voice and video calls.

Operating System: Runs on Android 10, providing access to essential apps and functionalities.

Display: Features a 3-inch screen suitable for basic tasks like web browsing and using apps such as YouTube and WhatsApp.

Camera: Equipped with a 5MP rear camera and a 2MP front camera for basic photography needs.
