SM7B Cardioid Dynamic Vocal Microphone


Key Features:

Flat, Wide-Range Frequency Response: Delivers exceptionally clean and natural reproduction of both music and speech.

Cardioid Polar Pattern: Provides excellent off-axis rejection, minimizing background noise and focusing on the sound source.

Air Suspension Shock Isolation: Virtually eliminates mechanical noise transmission, ensuring quiet operation.

Bass Roll-Off and Mid-Range Emphasis Controls: Allow for tailored sound to fit various recording needs.
